What is CodeQuest?

CodeQuest is an action-adventure game. Battle challenges, complete quests, and level up — all while learning IT concepts in a fun and interactive way.

Our purpose is to make learning IT engaging. Every action in the game teaches you something new about technology, coding, and IT history.

How to Play: CodeQuest

Getting Started

  1. Click the Download button to get the game.
  2. Install and launch CodeQuest.
  3. Choose your character.
  4. Complete quests, solve puzzles, and battle challenges to learn IT concepts.
  5. Track your progress and climb the Achievements!

Game Premise

CodeQuest is an action-adventure game where every quest teaches you something new about technology, coding, and IT history. Level up your IT knowledge while having fun!
